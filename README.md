# Curate

A generative radio-station iOS app that creates personalized music streams based on songs, artists, genres, decades, moods, and fitness activities.

## Overview

Curate integrates with Apple Music and Spotify to let users discover and play music through various curation methods. The app features a swipeable interface with a custom bottom sheet input area for searching and selecting music.

## Features

- **Search-as-you-type**: Real-time search against Apple Music catalog with instant results
- **Multiple curation modes**: Curate music by Song, Artist, Genre, Decade, Activity, or Mood
- **Apple Music integration**: Play songs directly through the system music player
- **Spotify integration**: OAuth authentication support
- **Mood-based playlists**: Happy, Energetic, Calm, Romantic, and more
- **Fitness playlists**: Workout-focused music streams
- **Decade browsing**: Explore music through different eras
- **Genre exploration**: Discover music by genre categories

Music attribute data for generative streaming is provided by [GetSongBPM](https://getsongbpm.com).
